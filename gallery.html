Student Life at Our School

Beyond academics, our students engage in various clubs and activities that foster creativity, leadership, and teamwork.

Clubs & Societies

Eco Club, Science Club, Literary Club, and Cultural Club encourage active participation and creative expression.

Sports & Games

Students participate in athletics, volleyball, badminton, and yoga to promote fitness and team spirit.

Arts & Culture

Annual Day, Art Exhibitions, and Cultural Fests highlight the diverse talents of our students.

Social Service

Our students actively engage in cleanliness drives, charity events, and community outreach programs.
