Academics

At St. Joseph Girls Hr. Sec. School, we provide a well-rounded education combining academic excellence, moral values, and co-curricular enrichment. Our goal is to nurture each student's intellectual and personal growth.

Curriculum

We follow the State Board of Tamil Nadu curriculum from Kindergarten to Higher Secondary levels, focusing on holistic development and conceptual learning.

Subjects Offered

English, Tamil, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Computer Science, and Moral Science are part of our core curriculum. Students in higher classes can specialize in Science, Commerce, or Arts streams.

Facilities

We provide modern classrooms, well-equipped labs, a digital library, and sports facilities to support both academic and co-curricular learning.

Achievements

Our students have consistently secured top ranks in board exams and won numerous inter-school competitions in academics, sports, and cultural events.

Our Educational Mission

To inspire young girls to become confident, compassionate, and capable citizens who contribute positively to society. Our teachers are dedicated to creating a supportive and intellectually stimulating environment.

Our Learning Environment

We believe in learning beyond books. Our facilities ensure that students gain practical exposure, teamwork skills, and hands-on experience in every subject.

About Us

St. Joseph Girls Hr. Sec. School, Mannargudi, is dedicated to empowering girls through quality education and moral guidance.
